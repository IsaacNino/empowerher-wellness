What Makes Us Different

A Bridge Between Outpatient Therapy and Higher Levels of Care

EmpowerHer Wellness is a personalized outpatient service that combines individual therapy, life coaching, and family therapy to create a more connected and effective path toward recovery.

We were created to bridge the gap between traditional outpatient therapy and residential levels of care. Rather than requiring clients to participate in multiple group programs that may not address their specific needs, we tailor treatment around the individual and the realities of their daily life.

Sometimes individual therapy alone is not enough. Recovery requires support that extends beyond the therapy office and into the environments where life is actually happening. Through the integration of therapy and life coaching, we help clients apply what they are learning in treatment to their everyday goals, relationships, and challenges.

We also recognize that meaningful recovery rarely happens in isolation. Family members, trusted friends, sponsors, and other support systems often play a critical role in long-term success. Our family therapy component helps create alignment, accountability, encouragement, and sustainable support throughout the recovery process.

Most importantly, our team works together throughout the entire process. We do not outsource pieces of care to disconnected providers. Every part of the experience is designed to work together so clients receive personalized support that feels cohesive, practical, and relevant to their lives.
